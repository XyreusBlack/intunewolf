Experience Delivery

Day by Day

Each day has its own therapeutic role and its own cost center — making the program easy to understand, easy to budget, and easy to support.

Day 1 · Reality Center · Santa Monica

Nervous System Reset +
Community Activation

$32K
Experience Delivery

14 elders from the Ho-Chunk Nation arrive in Santa Monica and are welcomed into ceremony. The group moves through intimate sessions using a veteran-style integration framework, shared meals, grounding practices, and structured reflection before overnight lodging at Shore Hotel.

Morning
Welcome circle, cultural grounding, storytelling, and guided nervous system preparation.
Midday
Wave table sessions, binaural beats, vibration, and immersive sound therapy.
Afternoon
Howls That Heal pop-up, trust education, and a sensory preview of Day 2.
Evening
Journaling, shared reflection, dinner, and overnight lodging for restoration.
  • Intimate group rotations with personalized pacing
  • Trauma-informed facilitation throughout
  • Food, reflection, and communal support built in
  • Participants leave regulated and ready for Day 2
Day 1 Outcome
Participants feel safe, grounded, and prepared — nervous systems regulated, trust established, community formed.
Day 2 · Wolf Connection · Palmdale

Nature-Based Healing +
Wolf Immersion

$28K
Experience Delivery

After breakfast, participants are transported from Shore Hotel to the wolf sanctuary. A guided opening ceremony with the wolves, Reality Center meditation pads, nature immersion, emotional regulation exercises, and a sunset integration ceremony ground the experience in reflection, release, and renewal.

Morning
Breakfast, transport, arrival, orientation, and opening ceremony with the wolves.
Core
Guided wolf interaction, trust and boundary work, emotional awareness exercises.
Midday
Meditation pad activation, mindful walking, nature immersion, and small-group processing.
Sunset
Gratitude practice, symbolic release, collective sound release, and closing intentions.
  • Each participant receives a Reality Center meditation pad
  • Technology demonstrated in a natural, culturally resonant setting
  • Accessible to elders, at-risk youth, and broader community
  • Shows clear pathway for future community deployment
Day 2 Outcome
Participants leave with an embodied experience of trust, presence, and renewal — and a take-home tool to continue the practice.
